## How to Buy SYR

1. Go to [Uniswap](https://app.uniswap.org/).
2. Connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.).
3. Paste the token address:  
   `0xa7e21c9482dcc37af5f45715521209007372c828`
4. Swap ETH for SYR.
5. Confirm the transaction and add SYR to your wallet.
